WAP SITES

Because bandwidth is far less on a mobile phone, and because mobile phone screen sizes are only a fraction of what is on a computer, WAP sites are tiny compared to their larger brothers. PUSH SMS

We provide Push SMS services to companies that enable their consumers to request services such as ringtones or games from a wireless handset via sending a present SMS code to a predeter- mined number. With the current scale of mobile phone ownership, our Push SMS services help consumers connect with brands.

MOBILE APPS

Mobile Apps are a term used to describe Internet applications that runs with smartphones and other mobile devices. Mobile applications can help users by connecting them to Internet services more commonly accessed on desktop or note- book computers or help them by making it easier to use the internet on their portable devices. BLUE CASTING

Our services include Blue casting, a process in which a message broadcasts to Bluetooth-enabled devices within a given physical area. This technology is preferred by retail businesses, promoting sales in the mails by sending out discount coupons or relevant sales promotion offers.

QR CODE

Businesses of all sizes use QR codes in several ways. QR codes are used to enable consumers to connect your website containing the product details, find contact information and share your web URLs. They are added to business cards, flyers, and posters containing contact details to let consumers connect to specific products and services.
